What DonationConnect does

DonationConnect helps individuals and organizations donate or request essential items such as food, clothing, and medical supplies. The platform offers a simple flow to post donation offers or requests and helps match donors with nearby recipients during everyday needs or emergencies.

Who it's for

  • People who want to donate items to help others
  • Community groups or shelters that accept donations
  • Individuals seeking help or requesting specific items
  • Organizers and volunteers coordinating collection and distribution

How to use DonationConnect

1. Create an account — Click "Register" in the header and complete the form to create your free account.
2. Sign in — Use the "Login" button to sign in and access member features.
3. Choose a role — Select whether you want to donate or request assistance and follow the on-screen flow.
4. Post a listing — Add details about the item or need (description, condition, photos, pickup/delivery options).
5. Connect — Interested donors/recipients can message or contact you (this demo uses simple click actions; connect features can be added later).

Safety & recommendations

  • Verify recipient or donor details before sharing personal information.
  • Meet in public, safe places when exchanging items.
  • Prefer contactless handoff for hygiene and safety where possible.
